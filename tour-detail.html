About this experience

The Warner Bros. Studio Tour just outside London is the most comprehensive behind-the-scenes film experience in the world. Over 4 hours, you'll walk through original sets (the Great Hall, Dumbledore's office, the Gryffindor common room), examine thousands of original props and costumes, explore Diagon Alley, and step onto Platform 9¾ with the Hogwarts Express.
