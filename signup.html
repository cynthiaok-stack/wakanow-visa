Wakanow Affiliate Programme Terms and Conditions

1. Definitions. "Affiliate" refers to any individual or entity accepted into the Wakanow Affiliate Programme. "Commission" refers to the percentage of a completed booking's value paid to the Affiliate.

2. Eligibility. Applicants must be at least 18 years of age and provide accurate personal and business information during registration. Wakanow reserves the right to reject any application at its sole discretion.

3. Commission Structure. Affiliates earn commissions as specified in the programme tier they qualify for. Commission rates range from 5% to 15% depending on product category and booking volume.

4. Payment. Commissions are paid monthly via bank transfer to the account provided during registration. A minimum balance of NGN 5,000 must be accrued before a payout is initiated.

5. Registration Fee. A one-time registration fee of NGN 10,000 is required to activate an Affiliate account. This fee covers onboarding, training, and access to marketing materials.

6. Conduct. Affiliates must not misrepresent Wakanow's products or services. Any fraudulent activity will result in immediate termination and forfeiture of unpaid commissions.

7. Termination. Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice. Wakanow may terminate immediately for breach of these terms.

8. Limitation of Liability. Wakanow shall not be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from the Affiliate's participation in the programme.
